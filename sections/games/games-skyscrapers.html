Description:

Skyscrapers is a grid based game where the objective is to fill out all the cells with the correct numbers. You can deduce what numbers belong where due to the clues round the perimeter of the board.

The best way to think of these clues is to imagine you are stood where the clue is. When you face towards the board from this position, you can see the number of skyscrapers denoted on the clue. So if the clue says 4, then you can see 4 skyscrapers. This helps find the solution as the numbers you put in the boxes denote the height of a skyscraper. So you can see a 2 skyscraper infront of a 3 skyscraper but not vice-versa. The final rule to keep in mind is that skyscraper heights have to be unique to their row and column, much like in a game of Sudoku.
