The Activities page offers some suggestions on how you can spend your time in lockdown. This can range from active activities such as exercises and recipes but also some more passive activities like film and TV reccomendations.

The film and TV pages are made with the help of the TMDB API but the choices are personally chosen reccomendations in a number of set categories. In addition to the reccomendations, a randomised film and tv show is also provided which will hopefully go towards inspiring your next watch.
