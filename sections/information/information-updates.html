Charity Information

The COVID-19 pandemic has affected us all but sadly it has affected some more than others. Here is a list of links to some worthy COVID relief and other relavant charities:

  • World Health Organisation - Global COVID-19 Solidarity Response Fund
  • The Trussell Trust - Support for food banks in the UK
  • Age UK - Elderly care and support in the UK
  • Mind - Provide advice and support to anyone suffering with mental health issues
  • NHS Charities Together - A collective of over 250 NHS charities in the UK with the focus on supporting hospitals and their staff and volunteers

Advice

Even though the causes listed above are very important, here at Lockdown Lifesavers we realise that the first and most important thing to do is to look after yourself and those around you. Making sure that you personally are coping with the strange time we find ourselves in gives the basis to then being able to help others. For this reason we here offer some simple, easy to manage advice for dealing with each day during lockdown.

  • Eat regularly and healthily
  • Maintain a routine, including a regular sleeping pattern
  • Get some form of exercise every day
  • Get fresh air every day. If you are comfortable leaving the house for a period of time this can be as part of your exercise
  • Have an aim of something to achieve each day. This does not necessarily have to be conventionally productive but it gives you the mindset of having something to do for the day
  • Maintain social links. Whether this be phonecalls, zoom quizzes or responsibly meeting up with someone it is important to not let the world situation leave you isolated

Finally, and importantly, don't worry if you don't always follow the advice above. Above all it is important to get through the situation happily and healthily, we all deal with things differently.
